Our Story

Land for Life began in 2024 as a small group of students passionate about protecting forests and wildlife in their communities. Troubled by the rapid deforestation and habitat loss around them, they decided to take action by raising awareness, planting trees, and engaging local schools and families in environmental programs.

Over the years, our initiative grew into a full-fledged advocacy group, partnering with environmental organizations, local governments, and global networks. Today, we continue to fight for sustainable land use, conservation of biodiversity, and policies that protect future generations.

Who I am

I am a PSHS SMC scholar from the Philippines. I created this advocacy to spread awareness of the rapidly increasing danger that we are putting our land through, and to stop more destruction towards the living creatures and things in this world.
