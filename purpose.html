Land for Life
SDG 15: Life on Land

I built this website on SDG 15: Life on Land because I wanted to show how fragile and beautiful our forests, wildlife, and natural habitats are. By combining real stories, images, and practical ways to help, I hope to inspire people to care for nature and take small steps that make a big difference.

As a child, I used to watch birds nesting in the trees near my home, and over the years, I noticed fewer and fewer returning each season. This website shares that kind of story of how human activity affects the creatures and plants around us, and encourages visitors to protect the habitats that many species call home.
